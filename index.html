HOW IT WORKS

01
Collect
32 manager coins drop one by one during the World Cup. Each coin represents a real national team coach, launched on Solana via pump.fun.
02
Hold & Earn
Top 50 holders of each coin receive an airdrop of the next manager's coin before public launch. Hold to secure your spot.
03
Compete
Watch the leaderboard evolve in real time. Which manager coin reaches the highest score? Back your champion.
04
One Champion
The coin tied to the World Cup winning nation becomes the ultimate trophy. Community decides the legend.
